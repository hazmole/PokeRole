If a PokÃ©mon is fast and experienced it may only need a few seconds to perform many attacks in the same Round. A low rank PokÃ©mon might need the whole Round to perform only one attack.

Turns

A Turn is a fraction of the Round when a PokÃ©mon uses its action to perform a Move.

End of the Round

Once all PokÃ©mon have performed their respective actions and everything has been resolved, the Round ends.

Trainers have a chance to perform an action in this stage.

Some Moves and Status Conditions have effects at this point in the fight.
